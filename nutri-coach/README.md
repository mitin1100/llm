# 🥗 AI Nutrition Coach

AI Nutrition Coach is an intelligent nutrition assistant powered by the **Gemini 2.5 flash** multimodal model. The application helps users make informed dietary decisions by analyzing food images and providing detailed nutritional insights.

## 🚀 Features

### 1. Food Identification & Calorie Estimation

Upload a meal photo and let the AI automatically identify food items within the image. The system estimates the calorie content of each detected item, eliminating the need for manual food logging and providing a quick overview of meal energy intake.

### 2. Nutritional Analysis

Beyond calorie estimation, the application delivers a comprehensive nutritional breakdown, including:

* Protein
* Carbohydrates
* Fat
* Estimated nutritional balance

This helps users better understand the composition of their meals and track their dietary habits more effectively.

### 3. Personalized Nutrition Recommendations

Based on the meal analysis and nutritional profile, the AI generates personalized dietary guidance tailored to the user's health goals. Recommendations may include:

* Increasing protein intake for muscle development
* Adding more fiber-rich foods
* Reducing excess carbohydrates or fats
* Maintaining a balanced macronutrient distribution

## 🧠 AI Model

The application utilizes **Gemini 2.5 flash**, a multimodal large language model capable of understanding visual content and generating context-aware nutritional assessments.

## 🎯 Use Cases

* Daily calorie tracking
* Healthy eating guidance
* Weight management
* Fitness and muscle-building nutrition support
* General dietary awareness

## 📸 Workflow

1. Upload a food image.
2. AI identifies food items.
3. Calorie and nutrient estimates are generated.
4. Personalized nutrition recommendations are provided.
